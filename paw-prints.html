The Brief

A brand that says
love your pet.

Pāw Prints needed a brand identity that communicated warmth, trust, and professionalism to pet owners. The challenge was to stand out in a crowded market while feeling genuinely approachable — not corporate.

We developed a complete visual system from the ground up: logo, colour palette, typography, and applications across every physical and digital touchpoint. The golden retriever mascot became the heart of the brand — friendly, memorable, and instantly loveable.

Logo Guidelines

Clear Space

To maintain visibility and impact, the logo must always be surrounded by sufficient clear space.

Spacing Rule

The minimum clear space is defined by the height of the paw icon (X). No text, images, or elements should enter this area.

  • Maintain equal spacing on all sides
  • Do not crowd the logo
  • Always preserve proportions
