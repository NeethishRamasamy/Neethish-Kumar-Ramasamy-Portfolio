How the archive is organized

The page is arranged as a directory: first the overview, then the full grid of projects, then a short note explaining how the archive works.

  1. 01

    Overview first

    The hero gives a concise archive summary so visitors know what the page contains before they scroll.

  2. 02

    Project cards

    Each card links to the live project page, gallery, or case study so the archive remains actionable.

  3. 03

    Single visual language

    Even with varied project types, the cards keep a consistent rhythm, spacing, and CTA pattern.

  4. 04

    Open the work

    The archive stays lightweight: it points into the project pages instead of duplicating their content here.
