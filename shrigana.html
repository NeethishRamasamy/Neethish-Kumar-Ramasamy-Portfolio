Brand Story

Where nature
meets wellbeing.

A passion for optimism and holistic wellness gave rise to Shrigana. The company wants to make it possible for everyone to experience natural healing and personal development.

Shrigana promotes attentive living and honours the path to health and balance via its hospitable visual identity — a brand that feels as calming as the practices it stands for.

"The restorative power of nature and the optimism that comes with wellbeing are reflected in Shrigana's colour scheme — centred on calming greens with a hint of golden yellow. Brand materials always feel welcoming, organic, and fresh."

Logo Formation

Leaf + Sun = Life.

The dazzling sun element and organic leaf shapes stand for optimism, growth, and well-being. Paired with the Molgon Regular wordmark, the symbol feels grounded yet uplifting, while the symmetrical, flowing forms represent harmony, positive energy, and renewal.

The restorative power of nature and the optimism that comes with wellbeing are reflected in Shrigana's colour scheme, which is centred on calming greens with a hint of golden yellow. Building trust with a health audience requires harmony, growth, and a connection to organic living. In keeping with the brand's promise of radiant health and holistic treatment, the yellow sun aspect adds warmth and optimism. Brand materials always feel welcoming, organic, and fresh thanks to this soothing yet energising colour scheme.

Logo Guidelines

Clear Space

To maintain visibility and impact, the logo must always be surrounded by sufficient clear space.

Spacing Rule

The minimum clear space is defined by the height of the @ icon. No text, images, or elements should enter this area.

  • Maintain equal spacing on all sides
  • Do not crowd the logo
  • Always preserve proportions
