TT Group of Logistics

Year

2024

Project

Website Redesign

Project Overview

I recently had the opportunity to redesign TT Groups Logistics website, a international air cargo management, trucking, hotels & hospitality, insurance broking, and visa and consular services. The purpose of redesigning their webisite is to enhance user experience, improve navigation, and modernize the overall aesthetic while maintaining the core identity of the brand.

Problem

  • • Outdated Design:The previous design was not responsive and lacked modern visual appeal.
  • • Poor Navigation: Users found it difficult to find key information due to cluttered menus.
  • • Low Engagement: The site's static layout failed to engage visitors effectively.
Solution

  • • Responsive Design:Created a fully responsive layout that adapts seamlessly to various devices.
  • • Streamlined Navigation:Simplified menus and introduced clear calls-to-action to guide users through the site.
  • • Engaging Visuals: Integrated dynamic elements and high-quality imagery to enhance user interaction.

Research and Planning

The redesign process began with an in-depth research and planning phase to ensure the new website would meet the needs of its target audience. To identify the client pain points, preferences, and expectations interviews were held with them. Additionally, competitor analysis and stakeholder consultations helped establish benchmarks and define clear project goals. This phase also included analyzing existing website data, such as traffic patterns and user behavior, to pinpoint areas for improvement. The key problem points which are narrowed down from the client side are listed down as:


  • • A modern design that reflects professionalism and credibility.
  • • Easy navigation with clear CTAs for better user engagement.
  • • Mobile responsiveness for seamless browsing on any device.
  • • Faster loading speeds through performance optimization.
  • • Clear presentation of services with engaging visuals and concise content.

By addressing these needs in the redesign process, a website that not only looks great but also effectively serves its purpose as a business tool for TT Group can be created.

Wireframes

Once the requirements from the clients was collected, the focus now shifted to wireframing Wireframes were created to outline the structure of the website, showcasing the placement of key elements such as navigation menus, content sections, and calls-to-action. These wireframes served as blueprints for the design, ensuring a logical flow of information and intuitive navigation.
The User Experience (UX) Improvements which are made during the wireframe process are:

  • • Clearer Navigation:The redesigned site has a more intuitive and user-friendly navigation structure. This makes it easier for visitors to find the information they need.
  • • Clearer Navigation:The redesigned site has a more intuitive and user-friendly navigation structure. This makes it easier for visitors to find the information they need.
  • • Call to Actions (CTAs):The redesign strategically incorporates clear and compelling calls to action throughout the site, guiding users towards desired actions.
  • • Improved Content Presentation:The content is presented in a more concise and engaging manner. The use of headings, subheadings, bullet points, and visuals breaks up large blocks of text and makes the information easier to digest.
  • • Interactive Elements:The redesigned site may include interactive elements such as animations, hover effects, and sliders to enhance user engagement.
  • • Faster Loading Speed:Optimizations in the redesign likely result in faster loading speeds, improving the overall user experience.

Development

After getting apporval from the clients using the wireframes, the process was now shifted to developing stage. The website was developed using HTML5, CSS, and Javascript. To ensure a clear and meaningful structure for both users and search engines HTML5 was used. CSS3 was employed to get a animations, transistions, subtle engaging interactions, visually appealing and responsive layouts. JavaScript was integral in creating dynamic, interactive elements and also to enhance the user interactions. AJAX was also used for loading content dynamically without page reloads. The Design and Visual Improvements which are made during the Development stage are:

  • • Modern Layout and Structure:The redesign features a more contemporary and visually appealing layout. It uses a clear grid system, better spacing, and a more intuitive information hierarchy compared to the original.
  • • Improved Color Palette:The redesigned site employs a more sophisticated and consistent color palette. Though red colour (#eb1424) is also used in the original site's, the colors appear somewhat dated, while the redesign used the same red in the modern and harmonious scheme.
  • • Professional Photography and Visuals:The new design incorporates higher-quality photography and visuals. This instantly enhances the site's credibility and appeal. The original site's images are less impactful and too boring
  • • Typography:The redesign utilizes the same sans-serif font but it is used in a more modern and readable fonts. This greatly improves the site's readability and overall aesthetic. The original site's typography is less refined.
  • • Use of White Space:The redesign makes better use of white space, creating a cleaner and less cluttered feel. This improves readability and allows the content to breathe.
