A Personal Identity Project, Built From Zero

This project explores how I would build a bold, minimal identity system for a sports-inspired concept. The goal was to simplify the mark, sharpen the hierarchy, and make the visuals work across digital and print. "Less is more" became the guiding principle, and Deep Carmine became the signal.

The Brief

Simpler.
Stronger.
Clearer.

I wanted to simplify the identity, create a more flexible logo, and build a system that feels confident across every touchpoint.

The design philosophy is a 'less is more' approach — using restraint to create a personal portfolio piece that still feels energetic, memorable, and adaptable.

Colour System

Red Means
Business.

Deep Carmine
#B12C3B · RGB(177, 44, 59)
Primary · Passion · Energy · Strength. The dominant signal of the identity system — used on the most visible touchpoints.
Silver Chalice
#AAAAAA · RGB(170, 170, 170)
Secondary · Neutral · Professional. Supports the red without competing — used for backgrounds, text variants, and supporting graphics.
Black
#000000 · RGB(0, 0, 0)
Base · Authority · Discipline. The canvas for everything. Makes the red sing and keeps the palette grounded.

Using colour in graphic design is an excellent way to subtly influence how a viewer feels. Deep Carmine embodies the essence of sport — energy, strength, passion, and community. This choice aligns with the mission to inspire hope and drive within every athlete, making it an instantly recognisable and emotionally powerful symbol of the project.

The triad of Black, Carmine, and Silver creates maximum contrast at every scale — readable on a business card, unmissable on a motorway billboard.
