Project Overview

VON, a growing apparel brand targeting Gen Z, needed a bold, cohesive identity to reflect their edgy and expressive tone. I led the rebranding effort—from visual strategy and moodboarding to logo design and brand asset creation—to help position VON as more than a fashion label, but a youth-driven movement

Rebranding

The rebranding aims to simplify the brand's visual identity, create a flexible logo, strengthen the target audience, and transcend traditional sports merchandise. The design philosophy is a "less is more" approach, capturing the brand's heritage and core values. The new logo will be a storytelling tool, resonating with athletes and fans across generations. Expect a cleaner, adaptable visual identity and a brand that connects and inspires sports.

Typography

At the basic level, typography is the art that involves arranging a type-face in various combinations of font, size, and spacing.It is used in design to adjust the text within the design. In the VON brand logo "Oswald", a serif font is used because of the boldness , neat, discipline and straight forward attitude because sports needs lots of discipline and focus which is also much needed for a sports merchandise.

Colour

Using color in graphic design is an excellent way to subtlety influent the way a viewer feels about a composition. In that sense red colour is used in Von, by choosing Deep Carmine color which is red in simple words as a primary colour; it has been tried to embrace a powerful symbol that embodies the essence of sport—energy, strength, passion, and community. This choice aligns perfectly within the mission to inspire hope and drive within every athlete, making it an integral part of our brand identity as we move forward into this exciting new chapter. Also to support that Silver Chalice and Black is used a secondary colours.
